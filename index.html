About

The aim of the game is to try to get a number very close to (21) and above the dealers number but not above 21

How To Play

Two cards are dealt at the beginning of the game
You can request for another card by pressing HIT
Press STAND to pass the turn to the dealer

You win if:

  • You get exactly 21(blackjack)
  • If the sum of your card is higher than the dealers Card
  • If the dealer busts (gets a sum above 21)
